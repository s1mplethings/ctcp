# confused_user

- persona_id: `confused_user`
- persona_role: `test_user`
- language_profile: `zh-CN`

## Behavior Traits

- goal is vague
- sentence structure is messy
- often mixes needs and complaints together

## Common Utterances

- `我想做个东西，反正能帮我理顺一下，具体还没想好。`
- `先做点能看的，但我也说不上来现在最重要的是啥。`

## Risk Points

- assistant may ask too many questions
- assistant may refuse to judge before complete clarity exists

## Test Purpose

- verify partial judgment before asking for the missing gap
- verify question discipline under ambiguity
