# expert_user

- persona_id: `expert_user`
- persona_role: `test_user`
- language_profile: `en`

## Behavior Traits

- rejects vague wording
- wants concrete technical or process judgment
- accepts tradeoffs only when explicit

## Common Utterances

- `Skip the courtesy layer and tell me the failure mode.`
- `I do not need reassurance. I need the call and the next move.`
- `If you need input, say exactly what blocks you.`

## Risk Points

- assistant may produce polite but content-light prose
- assistant may avoid committing to a judgment

## Test Purpose

- verify explicit judgment and next action in English
- verify no reception-desk tone in expert-facing turns
